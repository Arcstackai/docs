1. Overview
ArcStack respects your privacy and your right to control your personal information. This User Data Deletion Policy explains how users can request deletion of their data collected through our Meta (Facebook and Instagram) applications.
We comply with Meta Platform requirements regarding user data access and deletion.
2. Data We Collect
When you use our Meta application, we may collect the following information, depending on the permissions you grant:
Public profile information (name, profile photo, Facebook/Instagram user ID)
Email address (if permission is granted)
Information necessary to operate our application
Basic usage and interaction data
We only collect data necessary to provide and improve our services.
3. How to Request Data Deletion
You may request deletion of your data at any time using one of the methods below.
Option 1: Remove the App via Facebook
To delete your data through Facebook:
Go to Facebook Settings & Privacy
Click Settings
Select Apps and Websites
Locate ArcStack
Click Remove
Once you remove the app, Meta will notify us and initiate the deletion process for your associated data.
Option 2: Request Deletion by Email
You may also request deletion directly by contacting us:
Email: info@arcstack.ai
Subject Line: User Data Deletion Request
Please include:
Your full name
The email associated with your Facebook/Instagram account
Your Facebook/Instagram User ID (if available)
This information helps us locate and securely delete your data.
4. Deletion Process
Upon receiving a valid deletion request:
We will verify the request to confirm identity.
Your personal data will be deleted from our active systems within 30 days.
Backup or archived data will be permanently deleted within 90 days.
You may receive confirmation once deletion is completed.
5. Data Retention Exceptions
We may retain limited information where necessary to:
Comply with legal obligations
Resolve disputes
Enforce agreements
Detect or prevent fraud or abuse
Any retained data will be limited to what is legally required.
6. Third-Party Service Providers
If your data has been processed by third-party service providers (such as hosting or analytics providers), we will take reasonable steps to request deletion of your personal data from those systems as applicable.
